About Us

VAMORE was founded with a simple yet powerful idea – to bring the heart and soul of American dining to Sweden.

Inspired by the classic diners of the U.S., we combine bold flavors, generous portions, and a warm, inviting atmosphere.

Our menu is a tribute to American culinary traditions, featuring everything from juicy burgers and crispy fries to indulgent milkshakes and slow-cooked barbecue. Every dish is crafted with high-quality ingredients and a passion for authentic taste.

But VAMORE is more than just food—it’s an experience. Whether you're here for a quick bite, a cozy dinner with friends, or a special celebration, we strive to make every visit unforgettable.

Welcome to VAMORE, where "America meets Sweden" and every meal tells a story.
